About Lachadi Cleaning Service

Lachadi Cleaning Solutions is a youthful limited company established and registered under the Company Act in 2018. We specialize in commercial and domestic cleaning services, fumigation services, and cleaning supplies.

We are fully compliant and hold all the necessary licenses and certifications required to operate in the cleaning industry in Kenya. Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction drives us to provide top-quality cleaning solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients.

Our Vision

To become the premier provider of exceptional cleaning solutions in Kenya, setting the industry standard for professionalism, reliability, and customer satisfaction. We aim to be recognized as the go-to company for creating immaculate and sanitized environments that promote well-being, productivity, and happiness for our valued clients.

Our Mission

To consistently deliver top-quality cleaning services, comprehensive fumigation solutions, and reliable cleaning supplies that surpass our clients' expectations. We are dedicated to deeply understanding and fulfilling the unique needs of each client, tailoring our services to provide personalized and meticulous cleaning solutions that ensure pristine, safe, and healthy spaces.
